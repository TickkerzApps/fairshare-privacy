FairShare

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2026
The short version: FairShare stores all your data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.

1. Overview

FairShare ("the App") is a household bill-splitting calculator developed by Tickkerz. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of FairShare on Android devices.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. FairShare is designed to work entirely offline — your data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

FairShare does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Names or personal identifiers
  • Financial information or income data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into FairShare — including names, income figures, and bill amounts — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using Android's standard AsyncStorage mechanism.

This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Accessible only by the FairShare app on your device
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Internet Access

FairShare does not require an internet connection to function. The App does not make any network requests. No data is sent to or received from any external server during normal use.

5. Third-Party Services

FairShare does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, or any similar service.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your data with any third parties. There is no data to share — all information remains on your device.

The App includes an optional "Export Report" feature that allows you to share a report via your device's native share sheet (e.g. email, messaging apps). This sharing is entirely initiated and controlled by you. FairShare does not have access to where you choose to send this information.

7. Children's Privacy

FairShare is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the App does not collect any personal information from any users, this is satisfied by design.

8. Security

Since FairShare stores data locally on your device and does not transmit data over the internet, the security of your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device secured with a PIN, password, or biometric lock.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
